Reptrack is a workout tracking app developed by Caffeinatedsoftwaresolutions. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter stays on your device.
If you use the optional cloud backup feature, your data is sent directly to your own cloud storage account (e.g. Google Drive). We have no access to it.
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13.
To delete your data, uninstall the App or clear its data via your device settings.
